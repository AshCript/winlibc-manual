.TH "RST2XETEX" 1 "" "" "text processing"
.SH NAME
rst2xetex \- convert reST documents to XeLaTeX
.SH SYNOPSIS
.sp
rst2xetex [options] [<source> [<destination>]]
.SH DESCRIPTION
.sp
Generates LaTeX documents from standalone reStructuredText sources for
compilation with the Unicode\-aware TeX variants XeLaTeX or LuaLaTeX. Reads
from <source> (default is stdin) and writes to <destination> (default is
stdout).  See <\fI\%https://docutils.sourceforge.io/docs/user/latex.html\fP> for the
full reference.
