.TH GCC-ar-12 1 "May 8, 2012" gcc-ar-12 ""
.SH NAME
gcc-ar \- a wrapper around ar adding the --plugin option

.SH SYNOPSIS
gcc-ar [\fB\s-1OPTION\s0\fR] ... [\fI\s-1ARGS\s0\fR...]

.SH DESCRIPTION

\fBgcc-ar\fR is a wrapper around ar(1) adding the appropriate
\fB\-\-plugin\fR option for the GCC 12 compiler.

.SH OPTIONS
See ar(1) for a list of options that ar understands.

.SH "SEE ALSO"
.BR ar(1)
