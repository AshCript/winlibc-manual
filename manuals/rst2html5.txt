.SH NAME
rst2html5 \- convert reST documents to HTML 5
.SH SYNOPSIS
.sp

.SH DESCRIPTION
.sp

<source> (default is stdin) and writes to <destination> (default is stdout).
See \fI\%https://docutils.sourceforge.io/docs/user/config.html\fP for a detailed
settings reference.
