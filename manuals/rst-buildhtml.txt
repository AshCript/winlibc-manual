.TH "RST-BUILDHTML" 1 "" "" "text processing"
.SH NAME
rst-buildhtml \- convert many reST documents to HTML
.SH SYNOPSIS
.sp
rst\-buildhtml [options] [<directory> ...]
.SH DESCRIPTION
.sp
Generates .html from all the reStructuredText .txt files (including PEPs) in
each <directory> (default is the current directory).
